CANine Updater

Upgrade your CANine firmware to the latest version

  • Unplug your CANine if plugged in
  • Move the BOOT switch to the ON position
  • Plug your CANine in
  • Press "Connect and Update" below
  • Wait for flashing to finish, then unplug your CANine
  • Unplug your CANine
  • Move the BOOT switch to the original position
